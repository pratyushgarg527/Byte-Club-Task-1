About Us

So here's a bit about us. We are the Computer Club of Bal Bharati Public School, Pitampura. We are visionaries, who love to discuss and debate about tech and to make it a part of our life. We believe in working as a single unit, i.e. Byte and we do this by participating in various events and taking initiatives for social awareness through various projects.
